ABOUT AIMT


The American Institute of Management and Technology (AIMT), an academic subsidiary of The Global Knowledge Foundation (GKF), USA invites papers for its 2021 Annual Conference which will be held virtually during June 20-21, 2021. The conference will provide an opportunity for participants to share and discuss their research in all areas of Applied Mathematics, Management, Computer Science, Engineering, and Technology.





ABOUT GKF


The Global Knowledge Foundation, U.S.A, Inc. (GKF) was established in the year 2010 to operate exclusively for educational, scientific, and charitable purposes within the scope of Section 501 (c) (3) of the Internal Revenue Code. The GKF is being operated by scientists, academicians, innovators, creative individuals, and dedicated professionals across the globe.

Please visit www.gkfusa.org for more details.

MISSION


The mission of GKF is to redefine, redesign, and reorganize the knowledge, research, and education under the global multilateral perspectives.

VISION



  • To enrich and enhance the knowledge base of the world's budding professionals by reaching and pooling the unreached high-end professionals to make them accessible, and to create a world of real-time vision of knowledge.

  • To promote and reshape innovative research in the field of Engineering, Technology and Management by forming world wide network of academicians, researchers, professionals and entrepreneurs and to prepare young scholars to realize their potential by self- assessment.

  • To provide educational opportunities and mentoring for needy students from the U.S and developing countries.

  • To focuses on national and international level online talent search examinations to nurture the hidden talents of the world's budding and blooming learners to identify and explore the potential of young scholars and reward them.

  • To provide the platform that shapes the mind to think, interpret, imagine, invent, and afford the privilege to rise above mediocrity.

  • To design, develop and explore the tools and technologies in the form of online academic evaluations, publishing journals, organizing conferences, and felicitating individuals and institutions for their outstanding contributions which will be periodically conducted across the globe.
